What is the perimeter of a rectangle?
The perimeter of a rectangle is the distance around the rectangle.
How to calculate perimeter of the rectangle?
To calculate perimeter of the rectangle you should use the following formula:
perimeter of the rectangle = 2 * (width of the rectangle + height of the rectangle)
